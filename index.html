Background

About Me

Technical Theatre student specialising in sound design — moving from stage to desk, and loving every second of it.

I'm Louis, a BOA Stage & Screen Technical Theatre student in Year 12. My theatre journey began on stage, performing in productions such as Popstars' 90s Musical (2019), Oklahoma! (2020), Battle of Boat (2022), Bugsy Malone (2023), and The Wizard of Oz (2024). Over time I discovered my passion lay behind the scenes — especially in sound.

In 2025 I moved fully into sound with SOSage and Cookie Theatre Company, leading audio for live performances: programming and running sound effects, balancing levels, supporting rehearsals, and troubleshooting in real time.

Alongside this, I've built more than four years of experience at Alderbrook School, working extensively with the Behringer X32 digital sound desk. I've mixed live audio for We Will Rock You, Grease, The Wizard of Oz, High School Musical, and National Theatre Connections at the Royal & Derngate, Northampton.

Proficient in QLab 5 — building and running cue stacks, triggering effects, and synchronising sound with other technical elements, including managing inputs and outputs, EQ, and solving problems under pressure.
